ABOUT US

CINEGENCE is now in its fifth year since its inception in 2017, during this time we have worked on a versatile range of projects with varying production scales delivering only excellence in the Film Industry across the Globe. We have been set up with a vision to establish a movie/film production studio delivering premium and high-quality services in Production Management,VFX Solutions,Design & Motion Graphics and Media & Advertising as its key highlight.

We are a TPN Certified Boutique Studio based in the heart of the Indian Film Industry, Mumbai; the Centre for some of the best Film & VFX talents available in the world, with the skills and knowledge of working on some of the biggest Regional,Hollywood and International movies that were executed in India.

OUR VISION

To become a Premiere Visual Effects Service & Solution Provider for our clients around the Globe.


OUR MISSION

To create a resource pool of task specific industry relevant skilled talent across geographies and provide the best in class and most effective creative solutions for our clients in the shortest turnaround times possible and scalable as per needs.
To propel inclusion and ensure at least 50% of our staff are differently abled and meet the challenges of this industry.






The Trusted Partner Network (TPN) is a joint venture between two major entertainment industry associations, the Motion Picture Association (MPA) and the Content Delivery & Security Association (CDSA), the worldwide leaders in third-party entertainment industry assessments.

The team and facility at CINEGENCE is well equipped to handle Media and Film Production for globally renowned platforms and formats.

OUR TEAM

STEPHEN MASCARENHAS
FOUNDER & CEO

With more than 25 years of experience in the Media and Entertainment Industry, winning the National Award for his contribution to a paper on “Stem to Steam” for engineering students at the 3rd National Teacher's Congress held in 2019, at MIT World Peace University Campus Pune, India, as well as being the Chairperson of the Corporate Advisory Board of School of Design (Multimedia), Lovely Professional University, Stephen leads the success of CINEGENCE with great panache. The concept and ideology of CINEGENCE is the brainchild of Stephen that stems from his expertise and undying passion for the entertainment business. While exploring a career to be a chef he put in a lot of hard work and dedication to make ends meet, he discovered his love for Theatre which was the beginning of his journey with Media & Entertainment. He began working with Dinyar Contractor Productions as a junior artist in the mid 90’s and the journey continued with a 5-year stint as a sports commentator for ESPN Star Sports that started at the age of 20. Stephen re-started his career in Mumbai in 2001 when he moved to Famous Studios - House of Animation as a business development manager. In 2004 he joined Prime Focus as a business development manager and executive assistant to the C.E.O. of the company. It was at Prime Focus he was exposed to a plethora of new opportunities and initiatives. He went on to grow the company and help the company achieve different milestones during his 17-year journey while building the organization. As Head of Production (film) for Prime Focus in India, he delivered over 25 feature films including big Bollywood blockbusters like Raaz, Ghajini, Wake Up Sid, Paa, Blue, and Chandni Chowk to China to name a few. His outstanding performance allowed him to continue his success run to achieve bigger milestones for the company as he went on to become the Senior Vice President VFX & 3D Stereoscopic conversion at Prime Focus World (now DNEG), where he was the key person to set up Prime Focus 3D Stereo Conversion & Gener8 Media Services in India. During this phase, He delivered over 40 3D stereo conversion projects, including the Martian (advanced imaging society winner), Ant-Man and The Avengers: Age of Ultron, Guardians of the Galaxy (International 3D Society Award Winner), Transformers: Age of Extinction, Star Wars: Episodes i - iii and Gravity (International 3D Society Award Winner). Stephen is a visionary and a natural when it comes to leading a team, his sheer determination and dedication to enable people, technology and the future of filmmaking is what sets him apart. Evangelizing careers for people with disabilities in VFX, his vision led his team and him to train & place over 30 personnel with hearing & speech disabilities at Prime Focus. His mission to train and enable people with disabilities in VFx continues even today at CINEGENCE.
